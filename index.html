About Mehak-e-Hifazat

Mehak-e-Hifazat brings you handcrafted scented candles that combine the serenity of fragrance with the warmth of purity. Each candle tells a story of calm, crafted with love and care.

Our Signature Scents

Discover our three signature scents — calming Lavender, refreshing Lemon, and the soulful Indian Origin. Each blend is designed to evoke a unique emotion and ambiance.

Our Impact

At Mehak-e-Hifazat, sustainability is at our core. Our candles are eco-friendly, toxin-free, and crafted to create a positive impact — both in your home and on the planet.
